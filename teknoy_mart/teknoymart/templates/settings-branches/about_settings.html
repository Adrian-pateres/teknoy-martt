About CIT-U eCommerce Platform

The CIT-U eCommerce Platform is a secure and community-focused digital marketplace designed exclusively for the students of Cebu Institute of Technology – University (CIT-U). Built to enhance campus life and promote sustainable resource sharing, the platform enables students to conveniently buy, sell, and rent items within a safe, university-verified environment.

A key component of the platform’s security is its university email authentication system, restricting access to users with valid @cit.edu email addresses. This ensures all participants are verified CIT-U members, reducing risks associated with public online marketplaces.

The platform provides features like item listing, searching, filtering, rental posting, and an integrated messaging system for safe communication between buyers and sellers. These tools ensure smooth interactions and efficient transactions.

From a technical standpoint, the system is powered by modern technologies: Django for backend logic, HTML/CSS/JavaScript for frontend, Supabase for secure database management, and Render for cloud deployment. Together, they ensure performance, reliability, and ease of maintenance.

Vision

To become the trusted and innovative digital marketplace that enhances student life at CIT-U by promoting secure transactions, sustainable resource sharing, and meaningful community connections.

Mission

  • Provide a secure, university-exclusive online marketplace for safe transactions.
  • Empower students through an accessible platform for academic and personal needs.
  • Promote sustainability and resource sharing through buying, selling, and renting.
  • Strengthen community ties through safe and direct communication.
  • Deliver a modern, scalable platform using advanced technologies.

Core Values

  • Security & Trust – Ensuring all users are authenticated CIT-U students.
  • Community-Centered – Strengthening student connections and cooperation.
  • Sustainability – Encouraging reuse, rentals, and waste reduction.
  • Accessibility – Providing a convenient and intuitive platform.
  • Innovation – Utilizing modern tools like Django, Supabase, and Render.

Executive Overview

The CIT-U eCommerce Platform is a secure, cloud-deployed system that simplifies item exchanges among students. With buying, selling, and renting features supported by Supabase and deployed via Render, it ensures stable performance and secure operations. This platform represents an innovative solution focused on convenience, sustainability, and community building within the CIT-U campus.

System Overview

The CIT-U eCommerce Platform is an exclusive online marketplace for Cebu Institute of Technology - University (CIT-U) students. It allows students to buy, sell, and rent items within the campus community. To ensure security, only users with a valid CIT-U email address can register and log in.

Development Information

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript (with Django templates or optional React)
  • Backend: Python, Django
  • Database: MySQL, Supabase
  • Authentication: Django Authentication with university email validation
  • Deployment: Heroku / Vercel / Docker (optional)

Support Information

For technical issues or feature requests, students can contact the development team via the official CIT-U eCommerce support channel or email. The platform is maintained to ensure secure, reliable, and smooth operations for all users.

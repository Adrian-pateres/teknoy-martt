Introduction

At Teknoy Mart, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you use our platform.

By using Teknoy Mart, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We encourage you to read this document carefully to understand our practices regarding your data.

Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide you with a secure and efficient marketplace experience within the CIT-U community.

Personal Information

  • Full name and CIT-U ID number
  • Email address (CIT-U email preferred)
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Profile picture (optional)
  • Department and year level

Transaction Data

  • Product listings and descriptions
  • Purchase and sales history
  • Messages and communications
  • Reviews and ratings
  • Payment information (encrypted)

Technical Information

  • IP address and device information
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Access times and pages visited
  • Cookies and tracking data

Location Data

  • Campus location for meet-ups
  • Building and room preferences
  • Delivery location within campus
  • Approximate location for security

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for legitimate business purposes to improve your experience and maintain a safe marketplace.

  • Account Management: To create and manage your account, verify your CIT-U affiliation, and provide personalized services.
  • Transaction Processing: To facilitate buying and selling, process payments, arrange meet-ups, and maintain transaction records.
  • Communication: To send notifications about orders, messages, platform updates, and respond to your inquiries.
  • Platform Improvement: To analyze usage patterns, improve features, fix bugs, and enhance overall user experience.
  • Safety & Security: To detect and prevent fraud, enforce our terms, protect against prohibited activities, and maintain platform integrity.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, legal processes, and governmental requests.

Data Sharing & Disclosure

We Do Not Sell Your Data. Your personal information is never sold to third parties for marketing purposes. We only share data when necessary to provide our services or as required by law.

  • Within CIT-U Community: Basic profile information (name, department) is visible to other verified CIT-U users to facilitate trust and transactions.
  • University Administration: We may share information with CIT-U administrators for account verification, policy enforcement, and incident investigation.
  • Service Providers: Third-party services (hosting, payment processing, analytics) may access data strictly for operational purposes under confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information when required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and the safety of users.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

  • Encryption: All data transmissions are encrypted using SSL/TLS protocols. Sensitive data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption.
  • Secure Storage: Data is stored on secure servers with restricted access, regular backups, and comprehensive disaster recovery plans.
  • Access Controls: Strict authentication mechanisms and role-based access controls ensure only authorized personnel can access data.
  • Regular Audits: We conduct regular security audits, vulnerability assessments, and penetration testing to identify and fix potential issues.
  • Payment Security: Payment information is processed through PCI-compliant payment gateways and is never stored on our servers.
  • Incident Response: We have a comprehensive incident response plan to quickly address any security breaches or data incidents.

Your Rights & Choices

You have several rights regarding your personal information. We are committed to helping you exercise these rights.

  • Right to Access: You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you in a structured, commonly used format.
  • Right to Rectification: You can update or correct inaccurate or incomplete personal information at any time through your account settings.
  • Right to Erasure: You can request deletion of your account and associated data, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You can request that we limit how we use your data in certain circumstances while maintaining your account.
  • Data Portability: You can receive your data in a portable format to transfer to another service provider if you wish.
  • Communication Preferences: You can control what notifications and marketing communications you receive from us.

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience, analyze usage, and provide personalized content.

  • Essential Cookies (Required): Necessary for the website to function properly. These cannot be disabled as they are essential for security, authentication, and basic functionality.
  • Performance Cookies (Optional): Help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting anonymous information about usage patterns.
  • Functionality Cookies (Optional): Enable enhanced functionality and personalization based on your preferences and behavior on our site.

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings or using our preference center.

Introduction

Welcome to Teknoy Mart's Privacy Policy

At Teknoy Mart, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you use our platform.

By using Teknoy Mart, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We encourage you to read this document carefully to understand our practices regarding your data.

Your Trust Matters

We take your privacy seriously and are transparent about how we handle your data. This policy is designed to help you make informed decisions about sharing your information with us.

Information We Collect

What data we gather and why

We collect only the information necessary to provide you with a secure and efficient marketplace experience within the CIT-U community.

Personal Information

  • Full name and CIT-U ID number
  • Email address (CIT-U email preferred)
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Profile picture (optional)
  • Department and year level

Transaction Data

  • Product listings and descriptions
  • Purchase and sales history
  • Messages and communications
  • Reviews and ratings
  • Payment information (encrypted)

Technical Information

  • IP address and device information
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Access times and pages visited
  • Cookies and tracking data

Location Data

  • Campus location for meet-ups
  • Building and room preferences
  • Delivery location within campus
  • Approximate location for security

How We Use Your Information

The purposes for which we process your data

We use the information we collect for legitimate business purposes to improve your experience and maintain a safe marketplace.

01

Account Management

To create and manage your account, verify your CIT-U affiliation, and provide personalized services.

02

Transaction Processing

To facilitate buying and selling, process payments, arrange meet-ups, and maintain transaction records.

03

Communication

To send notifications about orders, messages, platform updates, and respond to your inquiries.

04

Platform Improvement

To analyze usage patterns, improve features, fix bugs, and enhance overall user experience.

05

Safety & Security

To detect and prevent fraud, enforce our terms, protect against prohibited activities, and maintain platform integrity.

06

Legal Compliance

To comply with applicable laws, regulations, legal processes, and governmental requests.

Data Sharing & Disclosure

When and why we share your information

We Do Not Sell Your Data

Your personal information is never sold to third parties for marketing purposes. We only share data when necessary to provide our services or as required by law.

Within CIT-U Community

Basic profile information (name, department) is visible to other verified CIT-U users to facilitate trust and transactions.

University Administration

We may share information with CIT-U administrators for account verification, policy enforcement, and incident investigation.

Service Providers

Third-party services (hosting, payment processing, analytics) may access data strictly for operational purposes under confidentiality agreements.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and the safety of users.

Data Security

How we protect your information

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

Encryption

All data transmissions are encrypted using SSL/TLS protocols. Sensitive data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption.

Secure Storage

Data is stored on secure servers with restricted access, regular backups, and comprehensive disaster recovery plans.

Access Controls

Strict authentication mechanisms and role-based access controls ensure only authorized personnel can access data.

Regular Audits

We conduct regular security audits, vulnerability assessments, and penetration testing to identify and fix potential issues.

Payment Security

Payment information is processed through PCI-compliant payment gateways and is never stored on our servers.

Incident Response

We have a comprehensive incident response plan to quickly address any security breaches or data incidents.

Your Role in Security

While we implement robust security measures, you also play a crucial role. Please use strong passwords, enable two-factor authentication, and never share your account credentials with others.

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

How we use cookies and similar technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience, analyze usage, and provide personalized content.

Essential Cookies

Required

Necessary for the website to function properly. These cannot be disabled as they are essential for security, authentication, and basic functionality.

  • Session management and authentication
  • Security and fraud prevention
  • Shopping cart functionality

Performance Cookies

Optional

Help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting anonymous information about usage patterns.

  • Page views and navigation paths
  • Load times and error tracking
  • Feature usage analytics

Functionality Cookies

Optional

Enable enhanced functionality and personalization based on your preferences and behavior on our site.

  • Language and currency preferences
  • Custom display settings
  • Recently viewed items
